About the journal

DentArt has been published in Poltava since 1995 and is devoted to the aesthetic aspects of direct and indirect restoration, orthodontics, endodontics, periodontology and surgery.

The journal is practice-oriented: modern technologies and materials, detailed clinical protocols, and master classes from leading specialists in Ukraine and worldwide. Approved by the Ukrainian Dental Association.

Our editorial board brings together specialists from Ukraine, Italy, the United Kingdom, Moldova, Georgia, Latvia and Lithuania.
